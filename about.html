About Hung Pham

My name is Pham Quang Hung, a Vietnamese Data Analyst passionate about helping organizations make the best use of their data.

From Utilization data and how to capture data validly, through data preparation, analysis and management, all the way to significant insights presentation. I can provide support to each step of the process to enable your organization in making evidence-driven decisions or shares insights with new clients. Data Visualization and machine learning are some techniques that I am using to achieve this. I also have a passion for math, computing, photography, sports and healthy lifestyle.
